Staying ahead of deadlines is the simplest way to avoid penalties and keep your refund on track. Here's what to put on your calendar this year.

## Individual filers

- **April 15, 2025** — Federal individual return (Form 1040) and any balance due.
- **Quarterly estimates** — April 15, June 16, September 15, and January 15 (2026).

If you need more time, an extension gives you until October 15 to *file* — but not to *pay*. Estimate and pay by April 15 to avoid interest.

## Small businesses

- **March 17, 2025** — S-corporation (1120-S) and partnership (1065) returns.
- **April 15, 2025** — C-corporation (1120) returns.
